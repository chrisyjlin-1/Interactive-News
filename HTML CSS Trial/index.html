Back of House at Cookology’s 2025 Pop-Up Restaurant

By Christina Lin

*story edited from previously published article*

Northwestern University's cooking club Cookology hosted their annual pop-up restaurant Thursday evening at Sheil Catholic Center.

A reservation for the club’s biggest event is highly coveted and often hard to get. Students who were able to attend experienced a three-course meal inspired by street food. The menu included items like:

  • Tacos
  • Chicken Satay
  • Hotteok

Each course was served in roughly 20-minute windows and the Cookology chefs rushed to keep up with the timeline.
